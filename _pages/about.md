I am a third year PhD candidate at the University of Warwick. I am a research fellow at the [Center for research in Economic Theory and its Applications (CRETA)](https://warwick.ac.uk/fac/soc/economics/research/centres/creta/), a (Junior Fellow at the Centre for Advantage in the Global Economy (CAGE)](https://warwick.ac.uk/fac/soc/economics/research/centres/cage/about/people/), and a [Research Student in the Experimental and Behavioural Economics Research Group](https://warwick.ac.uk/fac/soc/economics/research/research-groups/experimental-behavioural-economics/) at Warwick. My main research interest is in how people make decisions. I explore these questions with observational data, experimentally, and theoretically.
